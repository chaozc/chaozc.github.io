Research Experience & Selected Course Projects

Research: Image Sentiment Analysis

  • About:My research topic during my summer internship in IBM Research -- China
  • Task: Automatically predict the sentiment orientation aroused by images shared in social web.
  • Basic idea:
    • Leverage a statistic-based mining approach to extract sentiment-related concepts in different semantic levels.
    • Leverage a convolutional neraul network for the first time to classify the image into different semantic levels.
  • Submission:"Image sentiment analysis based on hierarchical semantic concept ontology and convolutional neural network" to International World Wide Web Conference 2015 PDF

Project: Android Application: Student Online

  • About: Course Project of Software Engineering, got full marks in the final presentation. I led the 5-member android development team
  • Background: Students need various information everyday, including personal information such as course schedule, and campus-related information such as school notifications and news. However, it is difficult for students to collect these information from different websits. Thus, an efficient and effective platform to integrate and deliver the information is neccessary
  • Functions:
    • Collect various information distributed in different websites
    • Allow students to login with their official student account
    • Once login, students could:
      • View personal informations
      • Set personal keyword preference to filter information
      • Mark and collect important informations

Project: AI system: Computer-Go

  • About: Course Project of Artificial Intelligence, beat another 15 AI teams without a loss.
  • Basic idea:
    • Based on Monte-Carlo algorithms, leverage UCT to implement the simulation. Each node in the tree represents a game-status, and records the winning probalility. simulate thousands of steps and pick up the step maximizing the winning probability
    • Start from the root node, pick up the child node maximizing the winning probalility to be the next step. When it comes to leaf node, randomly simulate thousands of steps to count the winning probability

Project: Mathematical Model Construction and Analysis: The Keep-Right-Except-To-Pass Rule

  • About: Problem A of Mathematical Contest in Modeling (MCM) 2014, we won Meritorious Winner(15%). I led the 3-member team.
  • Description: See Problem A in PDF
  • Basic idea:
    • Design and build mathematical model to simulate the traffic condition in real roads
    • Leverage MATLAB to evaluate and analyze the rule by simulating the traffic flow in different conditions
  • Report: PDF
